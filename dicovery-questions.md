## **Phase 1: Discovery — Answers for Harvest & Spice**

### **1\) What do you do in one sentence? (elevator pitch)**

**Harvest & Spice is a Bay Area catering company serving vibrant, Moroccan-inspired, plant-forward meals for retreats and gatherings—fresh, beautiful food that feels nourishing and memorable.**

(Alt shorter option)  
**Moroccan-inspired, vegetable-forward catering for Bay Area retreats and events—bold flavor, clean ingredients, and effortless dietary accommodations.**

---

### **2\) Who hires you?**

**My clients are retreat centers, wellness groups, and organizations hosting multi-day retreats, plus private clients and small companies looking for high-quality catering.**  
Typical events: **retreat weekends, corporate lunches, intimate dinners, celebrations, and community gatherings.**

---

### **3\) What’s your service area?**

**I serve the Bay Area and nearby retreat destinations in Northern California**—especially:

* Oakland / Berkeley / East Bay  
* Marin, SF, Peninsula (case-by-case)  
* Retreat locations like Ben Lomond / Santa Cruz Mountains / Sonoma

(If you want a clean line:)  
**Based in Oakland and available throughout the Bay Area \+ select Northern California retreat locations.**

---

### **4\) What makes you different?**

Use 3–5 of these bullets (these are strong differentiators):

* **Moroccan-inspired California cooking:** bold spice, seasonal produce, bright herbs, beautiful textures  
* **Vegetable-forward but satisfying:** plant-first menus with optional animal protein  
* **Dietary accommodations are built in:** vegan, gluten-free, dairy-free options that don’t feel like afterthoughts  
* **Food that supports the retreat experience:** nourishing, grounding, energizing (not heavy or fussy)  
* **Premium sourcing:** high-quality ingredients and clean flavor (not overly processed)  
* **Warm service \+ thoughtful details:** the food looks beautiful and feels intentional

If you want a single paragraph version:  
**Harvest & Spice specializes in Moroccan-inspired, vegetable-forward food that feels vibrant and nourishing—perfect for retreats. Menus are designed to be satisfying without feeling heavy, and dietary needs are incorporated seamlessly so everyone eats well.**

---

### **5\) What’s your price range or minimum order?**

You have two smart options depending on whether you want transparency.

#### **Option A (recommended): minimum \+ “starting at”**

**Minimum order: $1,500. Most retreat and event catering starts at $35–$60 per person depending on service style and menu.**

#### **Option B (keep private): no numbers**

**Pricing is customized based on guest count, menu, and service needs. Contact me for a quote.**

(You can also do “starting at” only)  
**Full-service retreat catering starts at $35/person per meal.**
